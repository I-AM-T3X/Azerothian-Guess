Azerothian Guess

A daily word challenge from the World of Warcraft universe.

  • This is a hangman-style game, not Wordle™.
  • Each day a new WoW word or name is chosen — guess it letter by letter.
  • Use the on-screen keys or your physical keyboard.
  • 6 wrong guesses and the poor adventurer meets their end.
  • Earn 1 point per correct letter, 10 points for guessing the full word.
  • Your score and streak are saved in your browser.
  • One game per day — the word resets at midnight.
